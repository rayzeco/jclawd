# HEARTBEAT.md

## Periodic Check Checklist (Minimal)

Daily / Frequent:
- Summarize key emails or action items when requested
- Track ongoing client or project priorities
- Highlight urgent strategic or delivery risks

Weekly:
- Review major initiatives (Rayze platforms, client engagements)
- Identify automation or productization opportunities
- Surface any operational bottlenecks or leverage gaps

As Needed:
- Deployment or infra sanity checks when working on technical rollouts
- Calendar or priority alignment summaries

Keep this lightweight and high-signal only.
